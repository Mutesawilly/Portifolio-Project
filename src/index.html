About Me

I am Mutesa Willy, an independent Rwandan Creative Front-End Developer with over 3 years of experience in modern web design and development. I specialize in building engaging, responsive, and visually compelling digital experiences using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS. My work has spanned collaborations with international clients, design studios, and creative startups, where I’ve consistently delivered high-quality, user-centered web solutions.

Academically, I am currently pursuing my Advanced Level studies at The Agahozo-Shalom Youth Village (ASYV), majoring in Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science. ASYV is known for its holistic education model that combines academic rigor with personal development, and I actively engage in its technical and creative communities. My academic interests include software development, visual computing, mathematics, and applied physics.

In addition to front-end development, I’ve explored areas like 3D modeling, Adobe Creative Suite, and UI/UX prototyping with tools such as Figma and InDesign. I’m also part of several tech and innovation clubs at ASYV, including Python programming, Byte Builders, and Jackal Tech, where I continue to build skills in coding, problem-solving, and collaborative innovation.

As a freelancer, I am currently open to remote collaborations and partnerships with forward-thinking agencies and creatives that value excellence, originality, and meaningful design.
